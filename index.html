About Us

Mothers Hangout Initiative is more than an organization ‚Äî we are a movement of women supporting women, building bridges of solidarity, and creating spaces where every woman can thrive.

We believe in peaceful coexistence among women, breaking down barriers, and fostering genuine connections that transcend social, economic, and cultural divides. Our work is rooted in compassion, driven by purpose, and sustained by the collective power of women who refuse to leave anyone behind.

"One for All, All for One"

Ubuntu: I Am Because You Are

At the heart of our work is the African philosophy of Ubuntu ‚Äî the belief that our humanity is intertwined. We recognize that when one woman rises, we all rise. When one woman suffers, we all feel her pain. This interconnectedness drives us to create programs that not only address immediate needs but also build long-term resilience and community strength.

We promote solidarity among women, giving voice to the voiceless, supporting widows in their time of need, and empowering vulnerable women to reclaim their dignity and independence.

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Mothers Hangout Initiative is a women support group aimed at promoting motherhood, peaceful coexistence among women, giving voice to voiceless women and widows, and building a society where peace, love, togetherness, and sharing are paramount.

We promote the ideology of Ubuntu ‚Äî the spirit of humanity: "I am because you are."

Our Mission

  1. To affect the lives of the less privileged and widows within and around us.
  2. To reach out to vulnerable women in our society.
  3. To comfort abused women and children by empowering them through skill acquisition and education.
  4. To add value to women's lives by making them mentally, physically, and financially independent.
